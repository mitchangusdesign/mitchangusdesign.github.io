Jazbay Grape

The Jazbay [JAZZ-bay] Grape flavor is a fictional ingredient from an RPG videogame called, Elderscrolls V: Skyrim. I initially used it as a placeholder when I was developing the brand identity. After some time and a few iterations, I grew to like it and opted to keep it.

Orange Zone

I chose ORANGE ZONE for this flavor for the element of danger the orange cones on the highway evoke. Orange Zone sounds cool and makes one imagine that big things like heavy construction or inclement conditions are happening around them when you "Enter the Orange Zone".

Death Punch

While it seemed risky at first, to give the punch flavor such an edgy name, after seeing the success of Liquid Death brand sparkling water, I think that the energy drink demographic can handle Death Punch.
