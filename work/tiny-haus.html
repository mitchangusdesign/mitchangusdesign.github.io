Tiny Haus Concept

Tiny Haus Logo and Marketing

Tiny Haus concept is based on the idea of tiny houses being made with high-end modern design. The branding is developed for a manufacturer who will attend trade shows and whose primary clientele is accustomed to clean, aspirational marketing.

Print Catalogue

The Catalogue for Tiny Haus is designed to mimic the look of an architectural magazine. With clean lines, bold contrast and clear, modern fonts, this catalogue communicates an air of honesty and stability. Exactly the message Tiny Haus wants potential clients to absorb.

Trade Show Exhibit Materials

Tiny Haus evangelizes its message of high quality builds and an attractive lifestyle on the trade show circuit. After all, with a name like Tiny Haus, larger than life imagery is a must to illustrate the massive value of making your own rules in your own space. 7 foot tall banner stands can be seen from a distance and invite the viewer to imagine their new lives in a Tiny Haus.
